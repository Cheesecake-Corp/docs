Cheesecake Corp helps you bake, ship, and manage cheesecakes at scale.

## Core concepts

- **Recipes** — Reusable definitions of a cheesecake.
- **Orders** — Requests to produce cheesecakes.
- **Deliveries** — Shipping and tracking.
